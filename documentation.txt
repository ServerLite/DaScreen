Window(screen_width, screen_height) -> (int, int)

Window().clear_screen() -> NONE | Resets the screens view.

Window().generate_border(id, position_x, position_y, size_x, size_y, symbol) -> (string, int, int, int, int, character) | Generates a non-filled square.

Window().generate_square(id, position_x, position_y, size_x, size_y, symbol) -> (string, int, int, int, int, character) | Generates a filled square.

Window().display() -> NONE | Displays the screen.
